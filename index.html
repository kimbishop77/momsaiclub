A free sample: Brand new to Claude? Start here.

Claude is the AI assistant I use most. If you've never touched it (or any AI), here's your first two minutes:

1 Go to claude.ai on your phone or computer — or download the Claude app.
2 Type a question the way you'd text a friend. Try: "I'm a busy mom who's never used AI. What are 5 ways you could actually help me this week?"
3 That's it. No manual, no settings. Just keep talking.

So what do moms really use it for? Here are four real examples. Copy any of these into Claude and watch what happens:

Dinner help

Try saying:

"What can I make with chicken, rice, and frozen broccoli in 30 minutes?"

School emails

"Read this long school email and tell me what I need to do, and by when."

Homework backup

Try saying:

"Explain fractions like I'm 9 years old."

Party planning

Try saying:
