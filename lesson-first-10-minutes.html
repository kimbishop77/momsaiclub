Your First 10 Minutes with Claude

What you'll do: have your very first AI chat — and leave with one real thing done for you.

You don't need to be "good with computers" for this. If you can send a text message, you already have every skill you need. Ten minutes, start to finish.

The steps

1

Open Claude

Go to claude.ai in your web browser, or download the Claude app on your phone. Sign up with your email — the free plan is plenty for now.

2

Say hello (copy this)

Your first message matters less than you think. But this one is a fun place to start:

COPY THIS PROMPT Hi! I'm a mom and I've never used AI before. Ask me 3 quick questions about my life. Then tell me 5 ways you could actually help me this week.

Answer its questions like you're texting a friend. Watch what comes back.

3

Learn the one skill that matters: the follow-up

Here's the secret nobody tells beginners — the first answer is just a first draft. You can talk back:

  • "Make it shorter."
  • "Explain that more simply."
  • "Give me 3 more options."
  • "That won't work for us because [reason]. Try again."

Pick one of the 5 ideas Claude gave you and say: "Let's do that one." Keep going until it's actually useful.

Honest note, mom-to-mom: AI sometimes gets facts wrong, with total confidence. For anything important — health, money, school deadlines — double-check before acting. Think "very smart friend," not "encyclopedia."

Make it yours

  • "Help me write a text to my sister — I need to cancel on her again and I feel bad."
  • "My 6-year-old asks why the sky is blue about once a week. Give me an answer she'll get."
  • "I have 20 minutes and no plan for dinner. Go."

That's it. You just used AI. Next lesson, we'll make it do something for you every single morning. 💛
